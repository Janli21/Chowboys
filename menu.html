How it works

Build Your Texan-Style Silog Meal

ChowBoys reinvents the traditional Filipino silog by combining it with Texas-style barbecue flavors. Every meal begins with our signature Texan Smoky Fried Rice, which is then paired with grilled or smoked meat toppings and finished with egg, sauces, or additional extras. Follow the steps below to build your bowl.

Order directly from this menu: select your Rice Base, choose 1 Topping, add Extras and Sauces, then pick your Drink and proceed to Checkout. Your cart updates live on the right (or tap the cart button on mobile).

Texan Smoky Fried Rice
