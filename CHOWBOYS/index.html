The Texan-Style Silog Experience

Traditional Filipino silog meals are built around rice, egg, and a savory meat topping—like tocino, longganisa, or tapa.

ChowBoys brings a modern twist by pairing that silog format with Texas-style barbecue flavor. We swap classic garlic rice for our Texan Smoky Rice, then top it with slow-cooked smoked meats or fire-grilled favorites and finish it with a fried egg.

It’s familiar comfort with a bold, smoky “yeehaw” kick—made affordable and made for everyday cravings.

Our Signature Texan Smoky Rice

Our signature base is smoky garlic fried rice seasoned with Texan BBQ spices and mild chili notes—a bold, savory foundation that stands on its own.

Every ChowBoys meal begins with this signature rice base before you choose your preferred meat topping.

Behind the Brand

Powered by Cultured Bistro Ventures

ChowBoys is operated by Cultured Bistro Ventures—an innovative food venture company blending Filipino comfort with international inspiration.

Cultured Bistro Ventures is organized as a general partnership composed of eight partners who manage and operate the business together. Our focus is developing fresh dining concepts that combine traditional Filipino cuisine with global flavors—while keeping meals affordable, accessible, and consistently satisfying.

ChowBoys is our playful Texan-Filipino fusion concept: smoky barbecue energy served in the familiar silog format Filipinos love.

Brand Identity

Our Brand Story

A cowboy spirit, a silog heart, and a bowl built on Texan Smoky Rice.

Lasap ang Yeehaw Sarap!

The ChowBoys logo features a cowboy-inspired horse mascot holding a bowl of Texan-style fried rice—capturing the fusion of Texas barbecue culture and Filipino silog dining.

The horse symbolizes adventure, energy, and cowboy culture, while the smiling expression represents our friendly and approachable dining experience.

The brand name “ChowBoys” combines the joy of enjoying food (“chow”) with the cowboy theme (“boys”), reinforcing the Texan inspiration behind every bowl.
